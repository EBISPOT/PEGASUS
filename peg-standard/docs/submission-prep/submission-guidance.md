# Data preparation
Before submission, prepare the three required files:
- [PEG evidence matrix](./peg-matrix-preparation.md) 
- [PEG metadata](./peg-metadata-prepare.md) 
- [PEG list](./peg-list-preparation.md)

When submitting online, you will also be asked to provide minimum required fields, including the PEG source and phenotype.
